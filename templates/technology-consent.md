## Acknowledgments

By signing below, I acknowledge and consent to the following:

### Benefits and Limitations
- [ ] I understand that online mediation uses technology to conduct sessions remotely
- [ ] I understand the benefits (convenience, safety through physical separation, access)
- [ ] I understand the limitations (technology failures, reduced nonverbal communication, privacy risks)
- [ ] I have been informed of in-person alternatives

### Privacy and Security
- [ ] I understand that the mediator will use reasonable measures to protect digital communications
- [ ] I understand that no technology is completely secure
- [ ] I agree to participate from a private location where I cannot be overheard
- [ ] I agree not to record sessions without written consent of all parties and the mediator
- [ ] I understand how my data will be stored, protected, and deleted

### Technical Requirements
- [ ] I have access to a reliable internet connection
- [ ] I have access to a device with camera and microphone (if video sessions)
- [ ] I am able to use the selected platform(s) or have received training
- [ ] I know how to contact the mediator if I experience technical difficulties

### Artificial Intelligence (if AI tools are used)
- [ ] I have been informed of the specific AI tools used in this mediation and their purposes
- [ ] I understand how AI influences the mediation process and at what stages it is used
- [ ] I understand the level of human oversight the mediator maintains over AI use
- [ ] I understand how my data is handled by AI systems, including storage and deletion
- [ ] I understand my data will not be used to train AI models without my separate consent
- [ ] I understand the limitations and potential biases of AI-generated information
- [ ] I understand I may request that AI tools not be used, and alternatives will be provided

### Safety (for cases with domestic abuse concerns)
- [ ] I confirm that I will participate from a safe, private location
- [ ] I confirm that the other party will not be present in my location during sessions
- [ ] I understand I can contact the mediator privately if my safety changes
