## ODR Standards Assessment

### Accessible
- [ ] AI tools are accessible to both parties regardless of technical ability
- [ ] AI tools accommodate disability, language, and cultural needs
- [ ] AI use does not create cost barriers for either party
- [ ] AI use does not limit either party's right to representation
- **Notes**: _______________

### Accountable
- [ ] Human oversight is maintained over all AI-based interventions
- [ ] The origin of AI-generated documents and outputs is traceable
- [ ] The proportion of human vs. AI decision-making is documented
- [ ] Audit trail of AI operations is maintained
- **Notes**: _______________

### Competent
- [ ] Mediator has training in the AI tools being used
- [ ] Mediator understands the benefits, limitations, and biases of each AI tool
- [ ] Mediator can explain AI tools and their use to both parties
- [ ] AI-generated information is independently verified before use
- [ ] AI data sets are relevant to the legal jurisdiction of this case
- **Notes**: _______________

### Confidential
- [ ] Data handling policies for AI are disclosed to parties (who sees data, how it is used)
- [ ] Party data is not used to train the AI model (or consent obtained if it is)
- [ ] Data storage and destruction policies for AI-processed information are documented
- [ ] Breach notification protocols are established for AI-related incidents
- **Notes**: _______________

### Equal
- [ ] AI tools are monitored for bias in process and outcome
- [ ] Neither party gains a technological or informational advantage from AI use
- [ ] Impact on marginalized or silenced voices has been considered
- [ ] AI tool selection accounts for potential empowerment/disempowerment effects
- **Notes**: _______________

### Fair and Impartial
- [ ] AI tools have been evaluated for known biases and accuracy
- [ ] Human oversight prevents AI bias toward either party
- [ ] Potential conflicts of interest from AI algorithms or developers are disclosed
- [ ] Authority bias from AI-generated outputs is actively managed
- **Notes**: _______________

### Legal
- [ ] AI use complies with applicable data privacy laws and regulations
- [ ] AI use complies with applicable AI governance regulations
- [ ] AI outputs are appropriate for the legal jurisdiction of this case
- [ ] Intellectual property protections are ensured for AI inputs and outputs
- **Notes**: _______________

### Secure
- [ ] AI platforms provide strong data integrity and privacy protections
- [ ] Data protection plan for AI tools is documented
- [ ] Data retention and deletion schedules are established
- [ ] Data breach protocols are in place for AI systems
- **Notes**: _______________

### Transparent
- [ ] AI use has been disclosed to all parties before the process begins
- [ ] How and when AI influences the process has been explained in accessible language
- [ ] The magnitude of AI's influence on option generation and decisions is disclosed
- [ ] Level and type of human oversight over AI use is communicated
- [ ] Sources of data used by AI tools are disclosed
- **Notes**: _______________
