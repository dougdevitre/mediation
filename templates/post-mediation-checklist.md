# Post-Mediation Checklist

> **Per 2025 Standards VIII, XII, XIII & 2005 Standards V, VIII**: Ensure all required steps are completed after mediation concludes — whether by agreement, suspension, or termination.

---

## Case Information

- **Case Number**: _______________
- **Mediator**: _______________
- **Case Outcome**: [ ] Full agreement [ ] Partial agreement [ ] No agreement [ ] Suspended [ ] Terminated
- **Date of Final Session**: _______________
- **Total Sessions**: _____
- **Total Hours**: _____

---

## If Agreement Reached (Full or Partial)

### Agreement Documentation
- [ ] Written summary of all agreed terms prepared
- [ ] Summary reviewed with both parties for accuracy
- [ ] Each party received a copy of the summary
- [ ] Parties reminded to have agreement reviewed by independent counsel
- [ ] Disclaimer included: summary is not a legal document until finalized by attorneys/court

### Outstanding Items (Partial Agreement)
- [ ] List of unresolved issues provided to both parties
- [ ] Referrals for unresolved issues: [ ] Continued mediation [ ] Attorney negotiation [ ] Court [ ] Other: _______
- [ ] Timeline for addressing remaining issues discussed

### Court Filing (If Required)
- [ ] Parties informed about court filing requirements in their jurisdiction
- [ ] Parties referred to their attorneys for filing
- [ ] Mediator's role in court process clarified (mediator does NOT file on behalf of parties)

---

## If Suspended

- [ ] Reason for suspension documented (confidential mediator notes)
- [ ] Parties informed of conditions for resuming
- [ ] Target date for reassessment: _______________
- [ ] Referrals provided: _______________
- [ ] Any interim agreements documented and shared

---

## If Terminated (No Agreement)

- [ ] Termination notice provided to both parties (see templates/termination-notice.md)
- [ ] Reason for termination documented (confidential mediator notes)
- [ ] Referrals provided:
  - [ ] Other mediators
  - [ ] Legal aid / attorney referral
  - [ ] Court self-help resources
  - [ ] Counseling / therapy
  - [ ] Domestic violence services (if applicable)
- [ ] Parties informed of their right to seek resolution through other means

---

## All Cases — Required Steps

### Confidentiality & Records
- [ ] Confidential notes secured per practice policy
- [ ] Digital files encrypted and stored securely
- [ ] Record retention schedule noted: Destroy after ______ years (per jurisdiction)
- [ ] No mediation communications disclosed to third parties

### Safety Follow-Up (If DV/Safety Concerns)
- [ ] Follow-up safety check completed with affected party
- [ ] Safety plan remains in place through any transition period
- [ ] Referrals to DV services confirmed/renewed
- [ ] Protection order status verified (if applicable)

### Financial
- [ ] Final invoice issued
- [ ] Retainer balance refunded (if applicable): $______
- [ ] Payment received: [ ] Yes [ ] Pending — follow up by: _______________

### Professional Development
- [ ] Case reflected on for practice improvement
- [ ] Any ethical dilemmas noted for peer consultation
- [ ] CE or training needs identified: _______________
- [ ] Case logged in practice management system

---

## Party Feedback (Optional)

- [ ] Feedback form provided to both parties
- [ ] Feedback received and reviewed
- [ ] Any actionable items noted: _______________

---

## Case Closure

- [ ] All checklist items above completed
- [ ] Case marked closed in records
- **Date Case Closed**: _______________
- **Mediator Signature**: _______________

> This checklist is for mediator use. Customize to your practice and jurisdiction. See references/13-suspension-termination.md for guidance on ending mediation.
