## Safety Measures for Mediation Sessions

### Before Sessions
- [ ] Separate intake sessions conducted (never joint)
- [ ] Staggered arrival/departure times established — Party A arrives at ______, Party B at ______
- [ ] Separate waiting areas arranged
- [ ] Security personnel notified (if in-person)
- [ ] Emergency contacts confirmed for affected party

### During Sessions
- **Session format**: [ ] Shuttle mediation (parties never in same room) [ ] Caucus-only [ ] ODR/video with separate virtual rooms [ ] Other: _______________
- [ ] No joint sessions without explicit safety assessment
- [ ] Mediator monitors for coercive control dynamics throughout
- [ ] Affected party has code word or signal to pause/end session: _______________
- [ ] Breaks offered regularly to reduce pressure
- [ ] Each party has phone access at all times

### After Sessions
- [ ] Staggered departure — affected party leaves first
- [ ] Affected party has safe transportation arranged
- [ ] Next session scheduled with both parties' safety in mind
- [ ] Follow-up safety check scheduled within 24 hours: [ ] Phone [ ] Text [ ] Email

## Communication Protocols

- [ ] Mediator reviews all proposed agreement language for safety implications

## Technology Safety (for ODR/Remote Sessions)

- [ ] No recording permitted by any party
- [ ] Affected party knows how to exit video session immediately
- [ ] Backup phone number established in case of technology failure
- [ ] Mediator confirmed that the platform supports separate breakout rooms

## Conditions for Continuing Mediation

Mediation will continue only if:
- [ ] Affected party confirms willingness to proceed at each session
- [ ] Safety measures are maintained throughout the process
- [ ] No escalation of threatening or controlling behavior occurs
- [ ] Mediator assesses that meaningful participation is possible

### Grounds for Immediate Suspension
- Any threat of violence
- Violation of protection order
- Party appears under duress or coercion
- Mediator determines safety cannot be maintained
- Affected party uses code word or signal
