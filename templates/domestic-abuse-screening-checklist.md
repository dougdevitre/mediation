## Coercive Control Indicators

- [ ] Pattern of domination through multiple forms of control

- [ ] Punishment for "violations" of imposed rules
- [ ] Extreme jealousy or possessiveness
- [ ] Control over appearance, friendships, activities
- [ ] Requiring permission for basic activities
- [ ] Weaponizing legal/court processes
