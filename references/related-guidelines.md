# Related Professional Guidelines & Standards

> These additional guidelines from AFCC and other organizations are frequently used alongside the Model Standards for Family Mediation. Mediators should be familiar with those relevant to their practice.

---

## AFCC Practice Guidelines

| Year | Title | Relevance to Mediation |
|---|---|---|
| 2025 | Model Standards for Family and Divorce Mediation | Primary mediation standards — this repo |
| 2022 | Guidelines for Parenting Plan Evaluations in Family Law Cases | Context for when mediation transitions to evaluation |
| 2019 | Guidelines for Parenting Coordination | Post-mediation parenting coordination |
| 2018 | Guidelines for the Use of Social Science Research in Family Law | Evidence-based practice in mediation |
| 2016 | Guidelines for Examining Intimate Partner Violence (Supplement) | Supplement to custody evaluation standards — useful for DV screening in mediation |
| 2012 | Guidelines for Child Protection Mediation | Specialized mediation in child protection cases |
| 2010 | Guidelines for Court-Involved Therapy | When mediators refer to therapy |
| 2009 | Guidelines for Brief Focused Assessment | Quick assessment methods |
| 2006 | Model Standards of Practice for Child Custody Evaluation | Distinguishing mediation from evaluation |

Source: [AFCC Practice Guidelines](https://www.afccnet.org/Resource-Center/Practice-Guidelines)

## ODR & Technology Standards

| Year | Title | Organizations | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| 2022 | Online Dispute Resolution Standards | NCTDR / ICODR | 9 interdependent standards: Accessible, Accountable, Competent, Confidential, Equal, Fair and Impartial, Legal, Secure, Transparent. Adopted as ISO 32122 (2025). Available at https://odr.info/standards/ and https://icodr.org/standards/ |
| 2026 | Guidance for Third Parties Using AI in Dispute Resolution | NCTDR / ICODR | Applies the ODR Standards to AI use by practitioners; includes practitioner self-assessment checklist |
| 2016 | Ethical Principles for Online Dispute Resolution | NCTDR | Foundational values for ODR standards development; living document for guidance when standards lag behind technological innovation |
| 2009 | ODR Standards (first edition) | NCTDR | Original ODR standards; superseded by the 2022 edition |

→ See `references/19-odr-standards.md` for full standards text, AI guidance, and practice checklists.
