What's Inside

Reference Guides

17 standards-based guides covering every phase of mediation — intake, screening, facilitation, agreement drafting, and practice management.

Practice Templates

10 customizable templates in Markdown and Word format: intake forms, screening checklists, agreements, parenting plan worksheets, and more.

Scenario Library

20 "What do I do when..." scenarios with standards-based guidance for challenging situations mediators actually face.

Interactive Tools

React-based Parenting Plan Builder (50+ provisions), Ethics Compliance Dashboard, and CE Training Tracker.

Workflow Diagrams

6 Mermaid decision-flow diagrams for intake, DV screening, child maltreatment, conflict checks, termination, and the full mediation process.

CLE Presentation

10-slide PowerPoint presentation ready to deliver at AFCC, state bar, NASW, or EAPA conferences.

Standards Coverage

Every resource is mapped to the applicable Model Standard with the correct level of guidance:

SHALL — No discretion to depart SHOULD — Depart only with strong reason

14 standards from the 2025 AFCC/ABA Family Standards + 9 standards from the 2005 ABA/AAA/ACR General Standards = comprehensive coverage of mediator obligations.
