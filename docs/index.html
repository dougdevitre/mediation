Interactive Practice Tools

Parenting Plan Builder

Select from 50+ provisions across 8 categories, add notes, and export a formatted plan. Saves your progress automatically.

Compliance Dashboard

Self-audit your practice against 46 compliance items across 10 standards areas. Get scores, gap analysis, and corrective actions.

CE Training Tracker

Log training hours, tag topics, and track coverage against the 7 required training areas in the 2025 standards.

For Parties — Save Time & Money

Mediation Readiness

Step-by-step preparation tool: identify issues, set priorities, reflect on children's needs, gather documents, and write questions for your mediator.

Financial Disclosure

Organize income, expenses, assets, and debts before mediation. Incomplete financial info is the #1 reason sessions run long.

What Else Is Inside

Scenario Library

20 “What do I do when…” scenarios with standards-based protocols for challenging situations.

Reference Guides

19 standards-based guides covering every phase of mediation practice from intake through ethics compliance.

Practice Templates

12 customizable templates in Markdown and Word: intake forms, screening checklists, agreements, fee disclosures, and more.

Workflow Diagrams

6 Mermaid decision-flow diagrams for intake, DV screening, child maltreatment, conflict checks, termination, and full process overview.

CLE Presentation

10-slide PowerPoint deck ready to deliver at AFCC, state bar, NASW, or EAPA conferences.

5 State Jurisdictions

State-specific modules for CA, FL, MO, NY, and TX mapping model standards to local statutes, reporting, and qualifications.

Standards Coverage

Every resource is mapped to the applicable Model Standard with the correct obligation level:

SHALL — No discretion to depart SHOULD — Depart only with strong reason

14 standards from the 2025 AFCC/ABA Family Standards + 9 from the 2005 ABA/AAA/ACR General Standards = comprehensive coverage.
